# Playground

<LinkedFormula>

$$
L^{CLIP}(\theta) = \mathbb{E}_t\left[\min\left(r_t(\theta)\hat{A}_t, \text{clip}(r_t(\theta), 1-\epsilon, 1+\htmlClass{term-tmjobu5dd}{\epsilon})\hat{A}_t\right)\right]
$$

The clipped loss takes the minimum of the unclipped objective and a clipped version, where the probability ratio is <Term id="tmjobu5dd" color="amber">constrained</Term> within epsilon of 1, multiplied by the advantage estimate.
